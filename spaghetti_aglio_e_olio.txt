Ingredients (2 Persons):
- 500 g Spaghetti
- 5 garlic gloves
- 100 - 150 ml olive oil
- parsley

Directions
1. Boil and salt water
2. Add Spaghetti, cook until al dente
	2.1 meanwhile: heat up olive oil in frying pan at low/medium temperate
	2.2 peel cut garlic gloves into thin slices
	2.3 add garlic to olive oil
3. Sieve Spaghetti, put in pan with oil and garlic and turn over until fully 
	covered in oil-garlic mixture
4. Serve in Pasta bowl, add parsley
5. Enjoy!
